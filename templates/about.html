About Me

Hello Users and Welcome to Gujarati FODMAP CookBook. My name is Pranita Gandhi and I am the creator of Gujarati FODMAP CookBook. Due to suffering from Crohn's disease and IBS overlap I follow a LOW-FODMAP diet. The LOW-FODMAP diet ensures my IBS symptoms are kept at minimal level. Being a Gujarati and Gujarati food lover I struggled to find FODMAP friendly recipes. Gujarati food is very rich in pulses, lentils and wheat. Hence adapting to a LOW-FODMAP diet is difficult. Hence I created Gujarati FODMAP Cookbook to bring together my favourite Gujarati dishes which are FODMAP friendly. As the site grows many more dishes will be added from the growing user network.

I hope you find making and eating the dishes enjoyable. We would love to hear from you. Feel free to please drop us an email at: pranitacoder12@gmail.com
